Send new lead data to a URL of your choice to integrate MySiteAuditor with another app.

How it works

When activated, the URL below will receive a POST containing the 'email', 'name', 'phone', and 'url' of each lead that submits your embed form. In addition, your 'secret key' (below) will be passed so that you can authenticate this submission.
